in all general modes you'll have different fruits
that affect your speed and size.


eating an APPLE will halven your speed and
increase your size by 3.


eating a BANANA will double your speed and
increase your size by 5.


eating a CHERRY will double your speed and
decrease your size by 2.


eating a GRAPE will not affect your speed but
increase your size by 2.


eating a KIWI will double your speed and
decrease your size by 5.
